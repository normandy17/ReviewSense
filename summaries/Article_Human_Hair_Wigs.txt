### Buying Guide

When purchasing a human hair wig, consider the following factors:

- **Purpose**: Determine whether you need a wig for everyday wear, special events, or cosplay.
- **Material**: Choose between synthetic and human hair wigs based on your styling needs and budget. Human hair wigs tend to look more natural and can be styled with heat.
- **Cap Size**: Ensure you know your head measurements. Most wigs come in various sizes, and a proper fit is crucial for comfort and appearance.
- **Maintenance**: Understand how much time and effort you're willing to invest in upkeep. Some wigs require more care than others.
- **Style Options**: Decide on the length, color, and style that best suits your needs. Many wigs come with additional styling options, so consider those as well.
